Shmeep List

Checklist for taking care of the sheep

Morning Checklist

  1. Feed ram about 7-8 scoops of food from previous night's pre-hydrated feed buckets.
  2. Feed the rest of the sheep/ewes with the remaining feed in bucket used in previous step.
  3. Ensure all water buckets are full.

Evening Checklist

  1. Feed ram about 7-8 scoops of food from previous night's pre-hydrated feed buckets.
  2. Feed the rest of the sheep/ewes with the remaining feed in bucket used in previous step.
  3. Swap out milk bottles inside barn for lambs with full bottles. Make sure that the slit in the rubber top is facing up, and is inline with the offset part of bottle.
  4. Bring in old milk bottles, rinse out.
  5. Hydrate dry food buckets for next day (2 buckets).
